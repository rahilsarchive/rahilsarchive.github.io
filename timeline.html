~ Rahil's Timeline ~

A timeline for the things I have made on the internet, a progress tracker if you want to call it that.
I made it more for myself than anyone else, but it could be a useful measurement tape for you too.



2022

June 19th 2022 - Version 2.0 of this website released. You can still access the original version for context, or to debate which one was better (Not many would pick the older one, I'm sure).

2021

December 30th 2021 - Nostalgia Candidate a collection of short stories/essays, might be a litmus test for bigger future projects.

May 30th 2021 - Rahil Blade Stories a new YouTube channel created. It was made for telling shorter stories.

April 10th 2021 - finding place (short film) released. Made the music for the film as well, available on all platforms.

January 8th 2021 - Started making "video essays" on things that interests me. Hopefully will try to upload on a weekly schedule. First video was on Celeste.

2020

September 11th 2020 - "the last brain cell" is a game I made in 48 hours for the Unexpected Game Jam. CONTAINS 9 ENDINGS!!!. It also scored the 53rd rank out of 175 total entries, so not too bad.

July 31st 2020 - "Rahil's Paradox" I was working on a game that I lost access to. Pls help me get it back.

June 23rd 2020 - MY BLOG... A place where I talk about upcoming projects or just some random interesting stuff.

June 1st 2020 - I created my website. A very important event, cause without it you wouldn't be reading this.

May 23rd 2020 - Started working on something new, tone wise its very different from anything I have made previously. It will be interesting to execute this idea.

April 22th 2020 - Podcasts/Audio Logs Created.

April 15th 2020 - "Songs To Quarantine To" Music EP released as a reaction to the quarantine present at the time. My favorite project so far.

January 31st 2020 - Static, a project for my final year college completed. My First Video game made in Unity. Unreleased to the world and will stay that way.

2019

June 4th 2019 - Dorian a short film released. Had multiple endings.

2018

December 25th 2018 - "Jones Blake" a musical Story about a boy chasing his dreams and stumbling along the way, released. This was supposed to be a 3 part project. Abandoned part 2 in 2019 cause the ending was too sad and I didn't want to spread that kind of message. (Part 1 also unavailable due to the same)

July 16th 2018 - System As Sysdba a single was released. It was about the education system. It was aimed at the education system, not the teachers.

2017

July 27th 2017 - Stopped creating gaming videos cause too busy with exams, decided to spend time in more creative, artistic skills.

June 22nd 2017 - A Late Night Walk: Beattape EP This one didn't get a lot of appreciation, but i love it a lot. The first song is a personal favorite.

March 21st 2017 - "Lonely Boy left alone in the rain" my first piece of music. I made it in 40 minutes. Its the most popular video on my channel. Sometimes not trying very hard can surprisingly be very effective.
