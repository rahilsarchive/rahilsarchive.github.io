~ Rahil's Timeline ~

A timeline for the things I have made on the internet, a progress tracker if you want to call it that.
I made it more for myself than anyone else, but it could be a useful measurement tape for you too.



2023

↪ September 15th - Release of my second short film 'A Climb'. It is accompanied with its own original film soundtrack.

↪ January 9th - New season of my podcast/audiologs started. Now in the audio entries format.

2022

↪ December 30th - Released a new song accompanied by a lyrical visualizer called 'Static Closure'. Also available on all major streaming platforms.

↪ December 2nd - Official deployment of Rahil City, a central space that contains all the different versions of myself. A place to view my professional work and other stories.

↪ June 19th - Version 2.0 of this website released. You can still access the original version for context, or to debate which one was better (Not many would pick the older one, I'm sure).
