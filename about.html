Rahil's Archive, this website was created on June 1st 2020 initially containing only the timeline page. The purpose was to create something that would act as a central point for all the projects I had made over the years and since they occupy spaces on so many different platforms creating a singular place to access them all was a necessity. Over time this small concept got expanded into a blog, a journal and then I wanted to add a few new pages that would streamline and ease the goal that started it all - "To provide easy access to all my works".

The website you are currently seeing is version 2.0, it includes several new pages and some much needed aesthetic redesign. The full list of changes is given down below.

Version 1.0 (Original) - Remains deployed as it showcases progression.



  • New minimalistic design style and content separation.

  • New "Footprint" page that contains links to all my major works across multiple different platforms.

  • New "Gallery" page. I have always had an interest in photography, it is also a storytelling medium that I have not used at all; so I am excited to try it out and learn along the way.
    Note: The timings mentioned below each photograph corresponds to the date/time it was clicked and NOT the upload date/time.
